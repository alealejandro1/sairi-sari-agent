# Roadmap

## Phase 1 - Data capture
- Telegram commands for sales and expenses.
- Validation and confirmation prompts.

## Phase 2 - Ledger
- Chart of accounts tuned for sari-sari operations.
- Cash, inventory, payables, and utang tracking.

## Phase 3 - Insights
- Daily summary (cash-in/cash-out, top products, low stock).
- Weekly financial health report in plain language.

## Phase 4 - Digital twin
- What-if simulations for restocking and price changes.
- Agent recommendations with confidence scoring.
